Elevating Trust In Your Data With Python: A Practical Guide

March 09, 2024

Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS)

Data quality is paramount in the era of big data, whether it is feature engineering for ML models or analytics for data-driven decision making. How can we ensure the integrity of data in our daily workflows? Python, with its robust libraries and frameworks, is a powerful ally in improving data pipelines. In this session, you will learn about Python-based methodologies and tools to effectively identify and resolve data inaccuracies, drawing parallels to established software engineering principles. This talk is designed to empower Python practitioners with the knowledge to instill trust in their data, enabling informed and reliable decision-making. If you have ever faced the challenge of presenting accurate data in dashboards or questioned the reliability of your data sources, this session is for you.
